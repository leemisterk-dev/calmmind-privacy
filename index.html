CalmMind Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

This privacy policy explains how CalmMind handles user data.

Information We Collect

CalmMind does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Local App Data

CalmMind stores favorites, completed sessions, mindful minutes, streak information, and the last session date locally on your device. This information is used only to show your progress inside the app. It is not sent to CalmMind or to any third party.

Text-to-Speech and Sounds

CalmMind uses Android's built-in Text-to-Speech and audio features to play meditation guidance, breathing cues, and bell sounds. CalmMind does not record audio and does not access your microphone.

Data Sharing

CalmMind does not share user data with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because CalmMind stores progress only on your device, you can delete this data by using the app's clear progress option or by uninstalling the app.

Accounts

CalmMind does not require or provide user accounts.

Children

CalmMind is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

CalmMind is designed to avoid transmitting personal data. Locally stored app data remains on your device and is handled by Android's normal app storage protections.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the app developer using the support email listed on CalmMind's Google Play Store page.
